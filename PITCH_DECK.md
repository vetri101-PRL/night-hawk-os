## Slide 6 — The AI Layer

### This is not a dashboard with a chatbot bolted on. AI is the operating system.

#### What the AI does:

**1. Morning Brief Generation**
Every morning, the platform generates a plain-English brief covering:
- What your key metrics did yesterday vs prior week
- Which SKUs and cities need attention
- Top 3 priorities for the day
- Watchlist items (early warning signals)
- One strategic question to focus the founder's mind

*Example output: "Berry Burst 2-Pack complaint rate hit X% — overlaps with unresolved PP 2-Pack issue — suggests systemic packaging failure. Declare status by noon."*

**2. Ask Strategist (Natural Language Business Intelligence)**
Type any question in plain English. The AI has full context of your P&L, inventory, complaints, expansion data, and decisions log. No SQL. No filters. Just ask.

*Example queries a founder can run:*
- "What's my most profitable SKU by city after platform fees?"
- "Which cities are showing demand signals for expansion this quarter?"
- "What would happen to my runway if I doubled ad spend in Bangalore?"
- "Summarise all open issues and decisions from last 30 days"

**3. AI Strategy Advisor**
Generates multi-section strategic analysis covering:
- Business performance diagnosis
- Competitive positioning
- Growth priorities ranked by impact
- Risk register with mitigation steps
- 90-day action roadmap

**4. Marketing AI**
Dedicated AI for digital marketing — analyses creative performance, recommends budget shifts, identifies audience segments with highest LTV.
